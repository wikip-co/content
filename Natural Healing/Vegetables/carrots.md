**Carrots** are a root vegetable, usually orange in colour, though purple, black, red, white, and yellow cultivars exist. Carrots are a domesticated form of the wild carrot, Daucus carota, native to Europe and southwestern Asia. The plant probably originated in Persia and was originally cultivated for its leaves and seeds.

## Healing Properties

### Antioxidant

- Carrot juice supplementation was associated with elevated serum vitamin E levels, a key fat-soluble antioxidant.[^1]

### Immune System

- Carrot juice supplementation was associated with higher levels of immunoglobulin G (IgG) and increased lymphocyte counts, indicating enhanced immune function.[^1]

### Gut Health

- Carrot juice supplementation positively affected fecal Enterococcus counts, suggesting a beneficial influence on gut microbiota composition.[^1]
