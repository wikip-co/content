**Oleuropein** is a type of phenolic bitter compound found in green olives, olive leaves, and argan oil.[^1]

## Healing Properties

- **Antioxidant and Anti-inflammatory:** Oleuropein is one of the key polyphenolic compounds in olive oil that acts by scavenging reactive oxygen species, manipulating inflammatory pathways, and enhancing lipid and glucose metabolism.[^2]
- **Metabolic & Cardiovascular Health:** It is linked to protection against oxidative stress, metabolic dysfunction, obesity, insulin resistance, diabetes, and cardiovascular disorders.[^2]
