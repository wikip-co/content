## Overview

Oleocanthal is a phenolic constituent of extra virgin olive oil. 

## Health Benefits

It has been linked with antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activity, manipulating inflammatory pathways and enhancing lipid and glucose metabolism (Ghazali 2026).
