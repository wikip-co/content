## Overview

Hydroxytyrosol is a key phenolic constituent of extra virgin olive oil. 

## Health Benefits

It has been linked with antioxidant and anti-inflammatory activity and lipid metabolism, scavenging reactive oxygen species and manipulating inflammatory pathways (Ghazali 2026).
